Usage Instructions

You will be presented with text snippets from various job postings gathered from a variety of sources.

Swipe right or click the green button check if the text snippet is a job skill.

Swipe left or click the red button cancel if the text snippet is NOT a job skill.

Continue classifying as many new text snippets as you like. This helps to improve our machine learning algorithm's predictive capabilities.

Thanks for your support and have fun!
